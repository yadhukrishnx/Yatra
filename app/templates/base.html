withYATRA

YATRA, is a robust platform designed to efficiently manage and showcase information about various tourist spots. It leverages Django REST framework to provide users with seamless CRUD operations—Create, Read, Update, Delete—on destination entries. Users can effortlessly add new tourist destinations, capturing essential details such as Place Name, Weather conditions, precise Location including State and District, a Google Map Link for navigation, relevant images, and comprehensive Descriptions. Each entry is meticulously organized to offer travelers and explorers a comprehensive view of potential destinations.
